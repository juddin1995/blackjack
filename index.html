Blackjack Instructions

Objective: Beat the dealer's hand without going over 21.

    Card Values: Number cards are worth their face value, face cards are worth 10, and Aces can be worth 1 or 11. Gameplay:
    1. Each player is dealt two cards. The dealer also gets two cards, but only one is face-up.
    2. Players take turns choosing to "Hit" (draw a card) or "Stand" (keep their current hand).
    3. Players can also "Double Down" to double their bet and receive one additional card, or "Split" if they have two cards of the same value.
    Winning:
    • If your hand value is higher than the dealerâ€™s without exceeding 21, you win.
    • If you get 21 with the first two cards (an Ace and a 10-value card), you have a Blackjack and usually win 1.5 times your bet.
    • If the dealer busts (goes over 21), all remaining players win.
